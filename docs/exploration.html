1.2.5 Per Capita

Per capita funding is doesn’t show the same dramatic drops between 2022 and 2023, suggesting that the drop shown in the previous plots is due to unreported data from some municipalities in the dataset. This is consistent with the fact that the fir source data are continually updated, and that municipal reporting lags of up to a year are common.
